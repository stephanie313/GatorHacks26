How it works

1 · Detect K-Means on six SDOH rates clusters ~30,000 ZIP codes into equity tiers.
2 · Prioritize Each popup highlights the top two local concerns above the U.S. median.
3 · Act Evidence-based steps connect food, coverage, and chronic-disease risks.
