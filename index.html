The problem

Health outcomes are not equal
across communities

Chronic disease and lack of insurance cluster in specific ZIP codes and states. Without seeing where disparities concentrate, public health teams, clinics, and policymakers cannot target interventions where they matter most.

🫁
Asthma burden

Respiratory disease rates vary widely by region — from environmental exposure to access to specialty care. Hotspots often overlap with pollution and poverty.

⚖️
Diabetes & obesity

Type 2 diabetes tracks with obesity, diet, and physical inactivity. Community-level prevention programs can reduce long-term chronic disease costs significantly.

🛡️
Coverage gaps

Uninsured adults delay care and rely on emergency services. High-uninsurance areas need enrollment outreach and community health center partnerships.
